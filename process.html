Our Process

Initial Research

After talking about Grace Hopper in class, it was a unanimous choice of who we should do this project on. Seeing her contributions to computer science, NASA, and many other spaces, it was hard to look for another candidate to present for this project. Grace Hopper has proved herself a staple through all she had done and accomplished

Past Assignments

Some past assignments such as the Project Charter and Project research have helped expand our knowledge of Grace Hopper and how we can accurately and appropriately pay homage to her achievements throughout her time. Although there have only been a few assignments as of now, we look forward to future assignments like these that will help us to continue our broad scope of knowledge.

Group Review

Throughout these assignments and the project all around, having a group that is so dedicated to not only receiving a great grade, but also towards representing Hopper properly, it has made this entire process so much easier. We have a system to hold each of us equally accountable for our faults, and what we may need to work on, while having positive communication and focusing on constructive criticism rather than strictly criticizing.
